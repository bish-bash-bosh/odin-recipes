Kale Smoothie

Back to dishes list

Description

Yup, this is the thing you start drinking daily once you've hit 30 years of age.

Give yourself a dose of vitamin C in the morning with this vegan green smoothie. Along with kale and avocado, there's a hit of zesty lime and pineapple

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls kale
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 lime, juicy one at that
  • large handful frozen pineapple chunks
  • medium-sized chunk ginger
  • 1 Tbps cashew nuts
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Put all of the ingredients into a bullet or smoothie maker, add a large splash of water and blitz. Add more water until you have the desired consistency.
